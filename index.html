🎓 How Construction Estimation Works

1

Measure Areas and Volumes: Every material estimate start with geometry — area for walls/floors, volume for concrete. Always subtract openings like windows and doors.

2

Apply Mix Ratios: Mortar and concrete are never pure cement. Standard ratios like 1:4 (cement:sand) determine how much of each material is needed per cubic meter.

3

Account for Wastage & Bond: Real-world projects add 5–10% extra for spillage, uneven surfaces, and mortar joint thickness (usually 10–20mm).

4

Convert to Usable Units: Cement is sold in 50kg bags, sand in local volumetric units (ela/brass), and tiles in piece counts. Conversion factors bridge the gap.

Wall Material Calculation

Wall Construction

Bricks = Wall Area ÷ (0.22m × 0.07m)
Cement (bags) = Wall Area × 0.03m × (1/5) × 28.8 bags/m³
Sand (ela) = Wall Area × 0.03m × (4/5) ÷ 1.308

Assumes 230mm × 70mm brick, 30mm mortar thickness, 1:4 cement:sand ratio. 1m³ cement = 28.8 bags (1440kg ÷ 50kg).

Plastering

Cement (bags) = Wall Area × 0.019m × (1/5) × 28.8
Sand (ela) = Wall Area × 0.019m × (4/5) ÷ 1.308

Plaster thickness assumed at 19mm with same 1:4 mix ratio.

Floor Tile Calculation

Tile Estimation

Tiles Needed = Floor Area ÷ (0.3m × 0.3m)

Standard tile size 300mm × 300mm (1ft). For 600mm tiles, divide by 2 pieces.

Mortar Bed

Cement (bags) = Floor Area × 0.02m × (1/5) × 28.8
Sand (ela) = Floor Area × 0.02m × (4/5) ÷ 1.308

Mortar bed thickness: 20mm. 1:4 cement-sand mix.

Adhesive

Adhesive Cement = Floor Area × 0.005m × (1440 ÷ 25)

Adhesive bed thickness: 5mm. Uses 25kg adhesive bags.

Concrete Calculation

Volume

Volume = Length × Width × Height (m³)

Manual Mix (1:2:2 ratio)

Cement = Volume × (1/7) × 28.25 bags
Sand (ela) = Volume × (2/7) ÷ 1.308
Aggregate (m³) = Volume × (2/7)
BRC = Area ÷ (2.2m × 6m)

1:2:2 concrete mix. 1 bag = 28.25 units. BRC standard size 2.2m × 6m. Prices per unit in RM.

Ready-mix Alternative

Ready-mix Cost = Volume × RM 300/m³

Use lorri gred 25 ready-mix as benchmark.

Paint Calculation

Coverage Rates (per litre)

Nippon EasyWash / Court: 107 sq ft / litre
Dulux EasyClean: 124 sq ft / litre
Jotun Essence: 113 sq ft / litre

Coverage based on 2-coat application on primed walls.

Calculations

Sealer = Surface Area ÷ Coverage Rate
Paint = (Surface Area × Coats) ÷ Coverage Rate

Sealer/primer assumed as 1 coat regardless of paint coats.

📐 Formula Reference Guide

Detailed breakdown of every equation used in the calculators. Understanding these lets you adjust for site-specific conditions.

WALL & PLASTER

Gross Wall Area = Length × Height (m²)
Net Wall Area = Gross Area − Window Area (m²)
Brick Count = Net Area ÷ Brick Face Area
Cement Volume = Net Area × Mortar Thickness × Cement Fraction
Cement Bags = Cement Volume × Density ÷ Bag Weight
Sand Volume = Cement Volume × (Sand Fraction ÷ Cement Fraction)
Sand Ela = Sand Volume ÷ 1.308

FLOOR TILES

Tile Count = Floor Area ÷ Single Tile Area
Mortar Cement Bags = Floor Area × Bed Thickness × Cement Fraction × Density
Adhesive Cement = Floor Area × Adh Thickness × Density ÷ Adh Bag Weight

CONCRETE

Concrete Volume = L × W × H (m³)
Cement Bags = Volume × (Cement Ratio ÷ Total Ratio) × Bags per m³
Sand / Aggregate = Volume × Material Ratio
BRC Quantity = Slab Area ÷ (2.2m × 6m)
Total Cost = Σ(Quantity × Unit Price)

PAINT

Surface Area = Wall Length × Wall Height (sq ft)
Sealer Litres = Surface Area ÷ Coverage Rate
Paint Litres = (Surface Area × Coats) ÷ Coverage Rate
Tip: Add 5–10% overrun to all material quantities for wastage, breakage, and irregular surfaces in real construction sites.
